## 1. Descripción General
Farmers often face inconvenience in switching on a water pump installed in remote farms. Microcontroller based circuits are used to solve this problem. This next project presents a cellphone-based remote controller for water pump. By calling the cellphone attached to the controller, the water pump can be directly activated.
